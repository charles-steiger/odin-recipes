Pork Chops with Chopped Brussels Sprouts

Description

This recipe is one of our family's favorites. Bone-in pork chops have great flavor and taste amazing with chopped brussels sprouts.

Ingredients

Note: Organic ingredients used if available

  • 2 - 0.5 lb bone-in pork chops
  • 20-30 medium brussels sprouts
  • 2 tbs. ghee
  • 2 tsp. garlic powder
  • 2 tsp. italian seasoning (oregano, thyme, sage)
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1 tsp. pepper

Steps

  1. Chop brussels sprouts. I highly recommend using a food processor for this step.
  2. Heat pan to medium low heat.
  3. Season pork chops by sprinkling garlic powder, italian seasoning, salt, and pepper on all sides of each chop.
  4. Tenderize pork chops with a fork, pushing the seasoning into the pork chop.
  5. Add 1 tbs. of ghee to pan followed by the pork chops once ghee is fully melted.
  6. Cook pork chops, flipping every 2-5 minutes, until a minimum internal temperature of 165 degrees Fahrenheit is reached.
  7. Remove pork chops from pan and add chopped brussels sprouts to pan with 1 tbs. of ghee.
  8. Cook brussels sprouts 5-7 minutes, or until your desired tenderness. We like ours slighly crunchy.

Enjoy!

This recipe will make approximately two servings.
